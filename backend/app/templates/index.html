How the System Works

A seamless 4-step process from issue detection to final execution.

📸

1. Report

Citizens capture and upload photos of road damage with GPS tags.

🧠

2. AI Detection

Our AI instantly categorizes the severity and type of road damage.

📋

3. Verification

SMC officials verify reports and approve work orders for contractors.

🛠️

4. Repair

Contractors complete repairs, and status is updated in real-time.

System Features

🏠

AI Detection

Automated visual analysis to identify pothole dimensions and depth with high precision.

📈

Real-Time Tracking

Live dashboard for citizens and officials to track the status of every reported issue.

📍

GPS Reporting

Precise location tagging ensures maintenance crews find the exact damage site instantly.

👷

Contractor Assignment

Automated dispatch system for contractors based on location, expertise, and workload.

👁️

Transparency

Public-facing data on completion times and contractor performance ratings.

📡

Smart Monitoring

Continuous health monitoring of critical junctions and bridge infrastructure.
